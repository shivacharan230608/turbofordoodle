INTRODUCTION:

  • -The cholas or the chozhas were a south indian dynasty.

  • -they ruled for almost 1000 years

  • -They were one of the worlds longest ruling dynasties.

  • -The earliest references of the cholas were found in the Ashokan inscriptions dating back to 3rd century

  • -Their dynasty was also one of the first dynasties to have a massive navy

  • -They were a part of the three main dynasties of south india, the other 2 being pandyas and chezhas or chelas.

  • -Together the 3 dynasties were known as the muventhar- mundru or mu meaning three in tamil and venthar meaning rulers

  • -The cholas also had a very vast empire

  • -At their zenith, they had control over entire south india
